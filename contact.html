Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I use a promo code?

Simply click "Get Code" or "Reveal Code" on any deal, copy the code, and apply it during checkout at the retailer's website. The discount will be automatically applied to your order.

Are the deals on PromoDropper really free?

Yes! PromoDropper is completely free to use. We partner with retailers to bring you exclusive deals and earn a small commission when you make a purchase, but this never affects the price you pay.

What if a promo code doesn't work?

If a code doesn't work, it may have expired or reached its usage limit. Try refreshing the page for updated deals, or contact us and we'll help you find an alternative offer.

How often are new deals added?

We add new deals daily! Our team works around the clock to find the latest offers from top retailers. Subscribe to our newsletter to never miss out on fresh deals.

Can I submit a deal or store for inclusion?

Absolutely! If you know of a great deal or would like to suggest a store, please contact us with the details. We're always looking to expand our offerings to help you save more.
